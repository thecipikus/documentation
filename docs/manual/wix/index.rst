TubePress for Wix
=================

TubePress is available to all `Wix.com <http://www.wix.com/>`_ websites via
the `Wix App Market <http://www.wix.com/blog/2012/10/introducing-the-wix-app-market/>`_. Since TubePress LLC manages
the hosting of TubePress, there is no installation, upgrade, or maintenance required!
